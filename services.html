Ice Cream Truck & Catering

Indulge in the sweet delights of King Cone beyond the walls of our beloved ice cream shop through our vibrant ice cream truck and catering services. Our dessert food truck brings the joy of King Cone to various locations and events, offering a mobile experience that delights both young and old. Whether it's a family reunion, corporate event, or a wedding celebration, our professional and friendly staff ensure that your guests savor the magic of our homemade ice cream creations. Trust King Cone's ice cream truck and catering services to elevate your event.

Flavors & Specials

King Cone stands proud with its renowned selection of over 40 mouthwatering ice cream flavors available daily, ensuring there's a tempting choice for every palate. Our inventive creations (available seasonally) such as Balmy Strawberry or Door County Cherry Cheesecake promise a delightful journey for your taste buds. Embracing the changing seasons, we introduce special flavors that add an element of surprise to every visit. Our commitment to inclusivity shines through with a range of dairy-free options, as well as gluten-friendly choices in our Jester Paletas section.

Homemade Ice Cream Process

Crafting our homemade ice cream at King Cone is a meticulous process rooted in quality and passion. We start with carefully selected high-quality ingredients, blending fresh dairy, premium cocoa, and real vanilla to create a velvety-smooth base for our diverse array of flavors. From churning to packaging, each step is executed with precision to deliver consistently delightful ice cream. Experience the result of decades of craftsmanship at King Cone in Plover, WI, where each scoop tells a story of dedication to excellence.
