Welcome to King Cone

Experience the finest award-winning homemade ice cream in Central Wisconsin! Nestled in Plover, WI, we take pride in crafting rich, velvety flavors using high-quality local dairy and precision craftsmanship.

Fresh Ingredients & Decades of Passion

From our premium vanilla and rich cocoa powder bases to our inventive inclusions, every single batch tells a story of dedication to excellence. We offer an impressive daily variety of hard-serve ice cream flavors alongside premium sundaes, malts, flurries, shakes, and crisp homemade waffle cones crafted to brighten your day.

Dedicated to Dietary Inclusivity

Everyone deserves a delicious scoop! Our daily operations feature an array of carefully handled dairy-free choices, as well as exceptionally refreshing gluten-friendly alternatives in our popular Jester Paletas category—ensuring an unforgettable, safe dessert adventure for every guest.
